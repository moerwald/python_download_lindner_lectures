Collection of all Lecture Modules of Manfred Lindner

Last actualized 2012-09-09

The following lecture notes have been created between 1995 and 2011 to provide 1.) an introduction into nearly all data communication topics (historical, legacy and modern) and 2.) a compact background reference for recalling already understood topics just on demand. I regulary improve the lecture notes (marked differently to avoid duplicate downloads). You can use these modules freely on your own risk 1.) for you private self-study purpose and 2.) for non-commercial education (e.g. universities, schools, ect.) as long as you take the course material as it is and do not change the material (especially authors copyright notice) in any way. Please contact me if you have other plans. Any constructive feedback on the material (e.g. errors found, mistakes, usefulness, enhancements, contributions, links) is welcome.
